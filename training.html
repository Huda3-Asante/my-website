Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before enrolling

⏱️ How long does the training take?

The training runs for 5 working days. Each day covers a different aspect of ice kenkey production so you learn step by step at a comfortable pace.

🛒 What do I need to start the training?

You will need all the ingredients for making ice kenkey. A full ingredients list will be provided to you after enrollment so you can prepare before the training begins.

💰 Can I sell after the training?

Absolutely yes! The training is designed to equip you with everything you need to start your own ice kenkey business immediately after completing the program.

🎥 Is the training recorded or live?

The training is recorded. This means you can watch at your own pace and replay any session as many times as you need to fully understand each step.

📱 How do I get added to the WhatsApp group after payment?

Once we receive your payment confirmation, we will add your number to the WhatsApp training group. Make sure the WhatsApp number you provide during payment is correct and active.

➕ Can I add extra flavors to my package?

Yes you can! You can add any extra flavor to your chosen package at an additional cost of GHS 50 per flavor. Simply mention this when you message us after payment.

🙋 What if I have questions during the training?

Questions are very welcome! At the end of every day's session there is a dedicated Q&A time where you can ask anything and get direct answers from the trainer.
